Module scape.proto

Module: super light-weight prototyping.

The metatable of a prototype always points to its parent. Delegation (rather than copying) is used to search ancestors for data and function members. Instantation is therefore generally fast but dispatch slows down for deep hierarchies. Fusion allows to flatten the hierarchy of ancestors. First-order instances are empty tables with a metatable pointing to the ancestor prototype. Tagging adds an identifier to the prototype which enables isa-checks.
